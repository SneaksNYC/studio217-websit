## How to Use This Template

1. Read the intent of each email before writing
2. Replace all `[BRACKETED]` fields with your brand's specifics
3. Keep the word count tight — under 150 words per body
4. Adjust tone to match your brand voice framework
5. Test subject lines before sending

## EMAIL 1 — Welcome
**Send:** Day 0 (immediately after signup/purchase)

**Subject:** `[You're in / Welcome to [Brand] / Something great just started]`
> *Write 2–3 subject line options. Pick the most human-sounding one.*
> Option A: ___________________________
> Option B: ___________________________
> Option C: ___________________________

**Preview text:** `[One line that adds to the subject without repeating it]`
> ___________________________

**Body:**
> Hi [First Name],
>
> Welcome. We're genuinely glad you're here.
>
> [Brand] started because [one honest sentence about the founding reason — not a pitch, just the truth].
>
> You're now part of a [community / group / family — pick what feels right] of people who [describe your customer in a way that makes them feel seen].
>
> Over the next few weeks, we'll send you a few notes — nothing noisy. Just things we think you'll actually find useful.
>
> In the meantime, [one simple next step: explore the site / reply with a question / follow on Instagram].
>
> Good to have you.
> [Signature]

## EMAIL 2 — Story / About
**Send:** Day 2

**Subject:**

> Option B: The real reason [Brand] exists
> Option C: ___________________________

**Preview text:**
> ___________________________

**Body:**
> Hi [First Name],
>
> Most brands tell you what they make. We'd rather tell you why.
>
> [2–3 sentences: the founding story. A real moment, a real problem, a real person. Not polished PR — honest backstory.]

>
> We don't always get it right. But we're committed to [one clear promise to the customer].
>
> If you ever want to know more — or just want to say hi — reply to this email. We actually read them.
>
> [Signature]

## EMAIL 3 — Value / Education
**Send:** Day 5

**Subject:**

> Option B: The [Brand] guide to [useful topic]
> Option C: ___________________________

**Preview text:**
> ___________________________

**Body:**
> Hi [First Name],
>
> We promised to send you things worth reading. Here's one:
>

> [Optional: 1–2 bullet points if you have a list]
>
> We share things like this [regularly / every week / in our community] because [reason that connects back to your brand values].
>
> [Signature]

## EMAIL 4 — Social Proof
**Send:** Day 10

**Subject:**
> Option A: What [Brand] customers say (in their own words)
> Option B: Here's what happened when [customer] tried [product/service]
> Option C: ___________________________

**Preview text:**
> ___________________________

**Body:**
> Hi [First Name],
>
> We don't love talking about ourselves — we'd rather let our customers do it.
>
> *"[Real testimonial or case study — specific, credible, not generic. Name + context if possible.]"*
> — [Name, context: e.g., "founder of a small bakery in Austin"]
>
> [1–2 sentences of context: what was the problem, what changed?]
>
> [Another quote or data point if you have one — or skip and keep it short]
>
> If you've had an experience with [Brand] you want to share — we're listening. Reply here or [leave a review / tag us on Instagram].
>
> [Signature]

## EMAIL 5 — Soft Offer
**Send:** Day 14

**Subject:**
> Option A: When you're ready — we're here
> Option B: One more thing before we go quiet
> Option C: ___________________________

**Preview text:**
> ___________________________

**Body:**
> Hi [First Name],
>
> We've sent you a few notes over the last two weeks. We hope at least one of them was worth your time.
>
> We're not big on pressure. So we'll just say this:
>
> If you've been thinking about [next step: trying the product / upgrading / booking a call / joining] — now's a good time. [One line on why: seasonal moment / new availability / just a natural invitation — not a manufactured urgency].
>
> [Optional: one simple incentive if appropriate — or omit entirely]
>
> Either way, thank you for being here. We'll continue to show up for you — just at a calmer pace from here on out.
>
> [Signature]

**CTA:** `[e.g., "Take the next step" / "Shop now" / "Book a call" / "Explore [product]"]`

## Sequence Overview

| Email | Day | Goal | Tone |
|-------|-----|------|------|
| 1 — Welcome | 0 | Set the tone, make them feel seen | Warm, brief |
| 2 — Story | 2 | Build trust through authenticity | Personal, honest |
| 3 — Value | 5 | Demonstrate expertise, give freely | Helpful, clear |
| 4 — Social Proof | 10 | Reduce doubt, show real results | Credible, grounded |
| 5 — Soft Offer | 14 | Invite action without pressure | Calm, confident |
